1996 - 9th Annual Maxwell House Blues Mug Night

1996 was a significant departure from previous mug designs. The design was a much thicker mug that actually holds a decievingly small amount of liquid. A blue background reminescent of the '89 mug wraps the body, and a silouette Kiel Center graphic highlights the center. Unlike any mugs before, this one details who the opponent was - the Winnipeg Jets. The trend of Coffee Mug Night games in April was continued this year - the Blues 80th game of the season.
