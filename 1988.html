1988 - 1st Annual Maxwell House Blues Mug Night

The 1987-1988 Blues season was the first season that the organization gave away coffee mugs to fans that attended the game. The mug itself does not display any information that indicates whether or not the club would be giving away mugs to fans on an annual basis. The mug is a simple wide style coffee mug with St. Louis Blues printed next to an 80s era Bluenote logo on one side, and a small red Maxwell house logo and the words Sponsor 1988 U.S. Olympic Team on the other. The latter is an indication of the year the mug was given to fans. It's unclear when or how fans obtained the mug. It could have been given out during the first couple weeks of March, as mug giveaway nights were scheduled during this time frame in the years that came after. The 1988 Winter Olympics in Calgary did however take place in the early weeks of February of 1988 - which could be an indicator that the mug was released to fans a bit earlier.
