1993 - 6th Annual Maxwell House Blues Mug Night

The Mug for this season was slighly smaller than the 90 and 91 mugs, but focused more on graphic design. Graphics of hockey sticks and pucks were included on the sides along with the Bluenote and Maxwell House Coffee Cup logo. A very colorful and clean mug.
