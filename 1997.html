1997 - 10th Annual Maxwell House Blues Mug Night

1997 began a long line of mugs that were navy colored. '97 was unique in that there was a second color style given away. The reason for this is still unknown to me. There was a white version of this mug set on a blue background along with a 'Blues Coffee Mug Night' pin given out this year. Perhaps this is the reason for the chunky text and design of the mug itself. Maxwell House came out with a new logo, which is reflected on this mug along with the match up date, the opponent, and 30th Anniversary banner. The game was played in February.
